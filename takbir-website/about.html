About Takbir Group School

Established with a vision to provide quality Islamic education combined with academic excellence

Our History

Takbir group School was founded in 1998 in Igbeba, Ijebu-Ode, Ogun State, with a mission to provide quality education rooted in Islamic values. What began as a small Islamic school has grown into a renowned institution known for academic excellence and moral upbringing.

Our founder, Mr(alhaji) Lawal Adebola Mikail, envisioned a school where children could receive both Western education and Islamic knowledge, preparing them for success in this life and the hereafter.

Also we started our secondary school in the year 2014 we advance from Primary to Secondary so as to improve the quality of education beyond the primary level but to Secondary level so that higher education with islamic learning can be given to our student

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities, curriculum, and teaching methods while maintaining our core Islamic principles and commitment to excellence.

Our Islamic Foundation

At Takbir Group School, Islam is not just a subject—it's a way of life. We integrate Islamic values into every aspect of our educational program.

Students engage in daily Quran recitation, learn Arabic language, study Islamic history, and practice the teachings of Prophet Muhammad (SAW) in their daily lives.

Our Islamic studies program includes Quran memorization (Hifz), Tajweed, Fiqh, Hadith, and Seerah, taught by qualified Islamic scholars and teachers.

We observe the five daily prayers, Friday congregational prayers, and celebrate Islamic festivals while respecting Nigerian national holidays.
