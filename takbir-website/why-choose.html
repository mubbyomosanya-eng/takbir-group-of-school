Why Choose Takbir Group School?

Discover what makes our school the best choice for your child's education and Islamic upbringing

Strong Islamic Foundation

At Takbir group School, Islamic education is at the core of everything we do. We provide:

  • Daily Quran recitation and memorization (Hifz program)
  • Comprehensive Islamic studies including Tajweed, Fiqh, Hadith, and Seerah
  • Arabic language classes for effective Quran understanding
  • Daily congregational prayers and Friday Jumu'ah
  • Islamic character building and moral education
  • Celebration of Islamic festivals and events

Our students graduate with strong Islamic identity and practice.

Academic Excellence

We combine Islamic education with outstanding academic performance:

  • Nigerian curriculum approved by the Ministry of Education
  • Excellent WAEC and NECO results year after year
  • Qualified and experienced teaching staff
  • Small class sizes for individual attention
  • Modern teaching methods and educational technology
  • Science laboratories and computer facilities
  • Library with Islamic and academic resources

Our graduates gain admission into top universities in Nigeria and abroad.

Holistic Development

We develop the whole child - mind, body, and soul:

  • Sports facilities for football, basketball, and athletics
  • Extracurricular clubs (Debate, Science, Quran, Arts)
  • Leadership training and public speaking
  • Community service and outreach programs
  • Career guidance and counseling
  • Health and wellness education
  • Creative arts and cultural activities

We nurture confident, well-rounded individuals ready for life's challenges.

Safe & Nurturing Environment

Parents trust us with their children because we provide:

  • Secure campus with 24/7 security personnel
  • Clean, well-maintained facilities
  • Healthy meals prepared in hygienic kitchen
  • Medical care with resident nurse
  • Anti-bullying policies and character education
  • Parent-teacher communication and involvement
  • Transportation services with safety measures

We treat every child as our own, creating a family atmosphere.
